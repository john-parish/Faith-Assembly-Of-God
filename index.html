About Faith Church

Our Vision

What to Expect on Sundays

Don't worry about getting dressed up — just focus on getting here! Sundays are a celebration. We will sing, pray, and share a Bible-based message. Expect service to last about 90 minutes and include fast and slow songs, announcements, offering, and a message.

We also regularly celebrate communion and share about missions efforts from around the world. Children in grades K–5 are encouraged to attend Sunday morning service; they stay with their families through most of the service and are dismissed to a program designed specifically for them. Nursery is provided throughout the entirety of the service.

Our Beliefs

What We Believe

The Inspired WordThe Scriptures are Inspired by God and declare His design and plan for mankind.
The TrinityThere is only One True God — revealed in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
SalvationEvery person can have restored fellowship with God through Salvation, trusting Christ through faith and repentance.
Baptism in the Holy SpiritThe Baptism in the Holy Spirit is a special experience following Salvation that empowers believers for witnessing and effective service.
Divine HealingDivine Healing of the Sick is a privilege for Christians today and is provided for in Christ's atonement.
The Second ComingWe believe in the Blessed Hope — when Jesus raptures His Church prior to His return to Earth.
Eternal LifeWe look forward to the perfect New Heavens and a New Earth that Christ is preparing for all people who have accepted Him.
We are a member of the Assemblies of God and the Iowa Ministry Network. View full statement of beliefs →

Children's Ministry

Faith Kids

Our mission, along with Growing Faith and Sharing Faith, is to partner with families in providing a foundation for their children and developing them into fully devoted followers of Christ.

Each week our children worship in the sanctuary with the adults and are then dismissed at 10:45 AM. Children will learn through interactive Bible lessons, purposeful play, discipleship activities, and age-appropriate games.
